Main School Block



Completed back in 2011, this masterpiece is the heart and soul of Ashesi. It is where Ashesi became Ashesi. Beautiful greenery, on point offices and state of the art lecture halls named after highly respected individuals...the Ashesi Main Building has it all. What more could a school want. The couryards and the amphitheatre serve as awesome places to host gatherings or just chill out.

If Knowledge is power, then the Todd Ruth Library of Ashesi is the sun. Shelving over hundreds of books, the Todd Ruth Library with it's awesome interior design, provides the school with all the know-to's, know how's and know-why's it would need to survive. Ever wanted to know how superman got his powers?...or how Bill Gates became the richest man in the world?...The Todd Ruth Library is the place for you.
